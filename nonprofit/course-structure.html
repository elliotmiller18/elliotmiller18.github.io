Format

Instructors and students will meet once weekly after working hours (PST) or on a weekend at the student's convenience. Lessons will be ~1 hour and will be one of:

  • A formal lecture explaining a concept
  • Pair coding sessions where students and instructors work on a material-related project together

Students are required to have a computer (Chromebooks, Mac, Windows, and Linux computers are all acceptable) to join meetings and develop on. While a personal computer is preferable, a school-managed computer will work as long as tutors or students have the ability to download required software.
