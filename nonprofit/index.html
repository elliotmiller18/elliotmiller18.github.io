What we are

We provide free one-on-one, instruction on the foundational concepts behind real software and computer systems. Along the way, students will get firsthand experience writing systems code - a challenging but highly sought after field of software engineering that involves solving the world's hardest problems. Operating systems, code embedded in spaceships, and the engines powering modern video games are all examples of systems software.

Today, ambitious students who want to learn systems programming are met with the following options:

  • Reading dense technical documentation on their own
  • Paying for expensive tutoring
  • Audit university classes without instructor support

Our goal is to combine the best parts of each:

  • Carefully selected readings from foundational computer science texts
  • Individual guidance from experienced tutors
  • The structure and pacing of a university-level course
All entirely free of charge.

Who we are

We are systems programmers with experience at Apple and Amazon who want to make systems programming more accessible to motivated students. Our goal is to support students who are curious, motivated, and excited to study the foundations of computing.
